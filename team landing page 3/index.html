FORMULA STUDENT ELECTRIC

ABOUT US

We are 4ZE Racing, the official student formula electric racing team of SRM University, based in SRM IST, Chennai. Our team consists of 60 highly dedicated and passionate undergraduate students whose main passion lies in engineering and achieving new possibilities. We specialize in making electric race cars for the International Formula Student/Formula SAE competition held at various locations worldwide. The word '4ze' represents our commitment to zero emissions and a sustainable environment. Our team is completely student-managed, with three domains—Mechanical, Electrical, and Corporate—working together to ensure our car competes at a global level, focusing on energy efficiency and innovation.

4ZE Racing

Our team 4ZE Racing was established in 2012 by a group of students from SRM University who wanted to design an electric vehicle. “4ZE” stands for “For Zero Emission”. We are the official Formula student electric team of SRM Institute of Science and Technology. The team aims to achieve this target through development of an innovative and collabrotive environment.

Our Department

The Department of Automobile Engineering at SRMIST, KTR Campus, established in 2004, strives for international recognition. In 18 years, we've excelled in teaching and research, offering industry-connected undergraduate and postgraduate programs that focus on technical expertise, problem-solving, and innovation.
